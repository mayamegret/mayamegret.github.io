I'm a marine biologist currently finishing my MSc at the Universidade do Algarve, where I study nudibranchs. I earned my BS in Marine Biology from UC San Diego's Scripps Institution of Oceanography, where field research first hooked me on this kind of work. I'm also a scientific diver, currently working toward my Divemaster certification, which keeps me in the water most of the time. Outside of research, I love traveling and I am an avid photographer, so here is where you'll find a lot of my underwater and travel photography!
